Submissions

Submissions are open to anyone from Monroe Community College.

All literary works need to be less than 1500 words and submitted to us in .pdf format.

All visual art pieces need to be submitted to us in .pdf or .jpg format, and no larger then 300 DPI at 18" x 24".

Submit your work to: submit@ckmag.org
Submissions via any other route (Facebook, Discord, etc) will be discarded.

Stay tuned for future updates about our submission guidelines.

Cabbages & Kings is one of the oldest student organizations at Monroe Community College.

Founded 50 years ago in 1968, our purpose is to showcase the creative talent of the students, faculty, and alumni here at MCC.

Come see what all the excitement is about and join us every Friday at noon in Building 4, Room 112.
