Welcome!

November 1, 2018

We're happy to introduce you to our new Cabbages & Kings website!

Here you will find posts about events going on with the club, digital issues that we have released, our submissions guidelines, and some frequently asked questions.

If you need to get ahold of us, please check out the contact information at the bottom of the page. You will find the current officers of Cabbages & Kings, as well as our office location and weekly meeting information. We can also be found on Facebook, Discord, or just reach out to us via email.

We are just getting started with the new site, and we have more to come, so stay tuned!

Cabbages & Kings is one of the oldest student organizations at Monroe Community College.

Founded 50 years ago in 1968, our purpose is to showcase the creative talent of the students, faculty, and alumni here at MCC.

Come see what all the excitement is about and join us every Friday at noon in Building 4, Room 112.
