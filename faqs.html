FAQs

Q: When do you meet?

We have meetings every Friday from 12-1pm in Building 4, Room 112 (the Mac lab just west of the theatre and Dolce' Aroma Coffee).



Q: I would love to help, what do you need?

We're always welcoming of anyone who wants to help make Cabbages & Kings awesome! Here are some of the skills we need regularly:

  • Graphic Design
  • Web Design/Development
  • Journalists
  • Writers
  • Bloggers
  • Editors
  • Marketing + Advertising
  • Social Media Co-ordinators
  • Event Planning

Cabbages & Kings is one of the oldest student organizations at Monroe Community College.

Founded 50 years ago in 1968, our purpose is to showcase the creative talent of the students, faculty, and alumni here at MCC.

Come see what all the excitement is about and join us every Friday at noon in Building 4, Room 112.
